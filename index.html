Scenario A

Negotiating Remote Days

Situation: You currently work in the office 5 days a week. You are having a meeting to discuss changing your schedule.

Task: Talk together and try to reach an agreement on a trial hybrid schedule.

A
The Employee
  • Goal: Ask to work from home on Thursdays and Fridays.
  • Mention: Better focus, no commute stress.
  • Compromise: Be willing to accept just 1 day if necessary.
B
The Manager
  • Goal: Ensure the team functions perfectly.
  • Mention: Concerns about communication and monitoring work.
  • Compromise: Agree to a trial period if terms are met.

A: "I'd like to request a hybrid schedule..." / "I find that I am much more productive at home because..."

B: "My main concern is team communication." / "How about we start with one day a week and review it next month?"

Scenario B

The Digital Nomad Plan

Situation: Two friends are discussing a radical idea: quitting their corporate jobs to move to Bali and work online.

Task: Discuss the pros and cons. Decide if you should do it now or wait a year.

A
The Optimist
  • Goal: Convince your friend to go immediately.
  • Mention: Cheap cost of living, beautiful beaches, ultimate freedom.
B
The Realist
  • Goal: Make sure you both think about the risks.
  • Mention: Unstable internet, loneliness, time zone differences with clients.

A: "Imagine working from the beach..." / "We could save so much money on rent!" / "Life is too short to sit in an office."

B: "That sounds great, but what about the time zone difference?" / "I think we should save more money first before making a decision."
